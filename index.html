“Circle gets the square” is a short movie on a very straightforward topic - circles. The spaces of the Arts Center at NYUAD are filled with different shapes, but it does not come to our mind to check their actual repetitiveness. A circle is regarded as a symbol of unity and perfection - the visual harmony of the Arts Center is supported by it.

This movie captures an unexpected occurrence of the ultimate geometric form in the interior space, and as the saying goes, the circle gets the square - the idea is not complex, but it provokes the viewer to look around and pay attention to little things.
